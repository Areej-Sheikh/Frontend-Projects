About Starbucks

Starbucks is a popular coffee chain in the United States. It was founded in Seattle, Washington in 1971 by Philip and Brian Murray, who served as the company's CEO and Chief Executive Officer. The company has since expanded to 240 locations across the country.

Located in downtown Seattle, Starbucks offers a variety of coffee beverages, including espresso, lattes, cappuccinos, and iced teas. The company also offers a wide range of beverages, such as smoothies, shakes, and milkshakes.
